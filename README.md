Blinkit Sales & Inventory Analysis

 Project Overview

This project analyzes Blinkit's sales and inventory data using SQL, Python, and Power BI. The objective is to evaluate sales performance, product trends, customer purchasing behavior, inventory status, and delivery efficiency to generate actionable business insights.

---

Dataset

- Blinkit Orders
- Order Items
- Products
- Customers
- Inventory
- Marketing

---

 Tools Used

- Python
- Pandas
- SQL (MySQL)
- Power BI

---

 Dashboard

The dashboard includes:

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Order Value
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Revenue by Category
- Revenue by Payment Method
- Top 10 Products by Revenue
- Customer Segment Analysis
- Delivery Status Breakdown
- Revenue by Store
- Interactive Slicers

---

 Key Findings

- Revenue showed noticeable monthly variations, indicating seasonal sales trends.
- Dairy & Breakfast and Snacks were among the highest revenue-generating categories.
- Most orders were delivered on time, reflecting efficient delivery operations.
- Premium customers recorded higher average order values than other customer segments.
- A small number of products contributed a significant share of total revenue.
- Card and Wallet were among the most preferred payment methods.

---

 Recommendations

- Focus promotions on high-performing product categories to maximize revenue.
- Improve the visibility and marketing of low-selling products.
- Maintain adequate inventory levels for top-selling products to avoid stock shortages.
- Reduce delayed deliveries by optimizing logistics and store operations.
- Introduce personalized offers and loyalty programs for high-value customer segments.
